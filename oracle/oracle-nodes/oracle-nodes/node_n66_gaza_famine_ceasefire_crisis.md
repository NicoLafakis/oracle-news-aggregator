- **Collapsed talks**: September, October, November rounds all fail to produce agreement
- **Regional dynamics**: Hezbollah ceasefire in Lebanon reduces pressure on Israel to compromise

- **ICC**: Arrest warrants issued for Israeli PM, Defense Minister, and Hamas leaders for war crimes

- **US position**: Military aid to Israel continues ($14B package), diplomatic protection in UN
- **Arab states**: Normalization with Israel frozen, humanitarian aid to Gaza increased
- **European Union**: Divided between member states, limited coordinated response
