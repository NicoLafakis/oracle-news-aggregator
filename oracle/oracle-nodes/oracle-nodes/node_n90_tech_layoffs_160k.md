### **Major Layoffs:**
**Amazon:**
- Cuts: Up to 30,000 corporate roles
- Divisions: Cloud services, HR, devices, retail, advertising
- Largest restructuring in company history
- Billions reallocated to AWS and AI

**Microsoft:**
- Cuts: ~15,000 (6,000 May + 9,000 July)
- Divisions: Sales, gaming, administrative
- AI Investment: $80 billion infrastructure

**Meta:**
- Cuts: ~3,600 employees
- Target: "Low performers"
- CEO Quote: AI replacing mid-level engineering tasks
- Focus: Metaverse and AI development

**Google:**
- Cuts: Thousands across cloud, devices, products
- Strategy: Retraining over pure reduction
- Investment: Increased AI R&D hiring

### **Industry Pattern:**
- **Total 2025 Cuts**: 160,000+
- **Worst Since**: 2009
- **Pattern**: Pandemic overexpansion correction + AI restructuring
